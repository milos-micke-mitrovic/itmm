### FAQ Section
- **Title (EN)**: "Frequently Asked Questions"
- **Title (SR)**: "[SR] Često postavljana pitanja"

#### Q1: What do I need to provide to get started?
**A (EN)**: "Not much. A rough idea of what you want, any text or images you'd like on the site, and access to your domain if you have one. Don't worry if you're not sure about details — that's literally my job to figure out."
**A (SR)**: "[SR] Ne mnogo. Okvirnu ideju šta želite, tekstove ili slike za sajt, i pristup domenu ako ga imate. Ne brinite ako niste sigurni oko detalja — bukvalno je moj posao da to rešim."

#### Q2: How long does it take to build my site?
**A (EN)**: "Basic sites take 3–5 days. Standard ones about a week. Larger projects 10–14 days. These timelines start after we agree on everything and I have your content. I'll give you an exact timeline before we start."
**A (SR)**: "[SR] Osnovni sajtovi traju 3–5 dana. Standardni oko nedelju dana. Veći projekti 10–14 dana. Rokovi počinju nakon što se dogovorimo i dobijem vaš sadržaj."

#### Q3: Can I edit the site myself after delivery?
**A (EN)**: "If you go with the Pro tier, absolutely — it comes with a CMS that lets you update text, images, and blog posts yourself. For Basic and Standard, I handle changes for you, either included in your revision rounds or through a maintenance package."
**A (SR)**: "[SR] Ako izaberete Pro paket, apsolutno — dolazi sa CMS-om za ažuriranje tekstova, slika i blog postova. Za Basic i Standard, ja se brinem za izmene."

#### Q4: What is hosting and do you handle it?
**A (EN)**: "Hosting is where your website lives on the internet — think of it as renting space for your site. Yes, I handle everything: setup, SSL certificates, renewals. You don't need to think about it. I offer three hosting packages depending on your needs."
**A (SR)**: "[SR] Hosting je mesto gde vaš sajt živi na internetu. Da, ja se brinem za sve: podešavanje, SSL sertifikate, obnove. Ne morate da razmišljate o tome."

#### Q5: What if I don't like the design?
**A (EN)**: "You see the design before I write any code. We discuss it, you give feedback, and I adjust. That's what the revision rounds are for. I've never had a client unhappy with the final result because we communicate throughout the process."
**A (SR)**: "[SR] Vidite dizajn pre nego što napišem i jednu liniju koda. Diskutujemo, dajete povratne informacije, ja prilagođavam. Za to služe runde revizija."

#### Q6: What are revisions and how many do I get?
**A (EN)**: "Revisions are adjustments after you see the result — changing a color, moving a section, swapping an image. Basic includes 1 round, Standard and Pro include 2. A revision round is not a redesign — it's fine-tuning. Extra rounds are €30–50 each."
**A (SR)**: "[SR] Revizije su prilagođavanja nakon što vidite rezultat. Basic uključuje 1 rundu, Standard i Pro 2. Runda revizije nije redizajn — to je fino podešavanje. Dodatne runde su €30–50."

#### Q7: What happens if I want changes after the site is delivered?
**A (EN)**: "Small things? Message me, I'll usually sort it out quickly. Ongoing changes? That's what the maintenance packages are for. Completely new features or sections? We scope it like a mini-project with a clear price upfront."
**A (SR)**: "[SR] Male stvari? Pišite mi, obično rešim brzo. Stalne promene? Za to služe paketi održavanja. Potpuno nove funkcije? To planiramo kao mini-projekat sa jasnom cenom."

#### Q8: Do you handle domain registration?
**A (EN)**: "I can guide you through buying a domain or do it on your behalf. The domain is always yours — I never hold a client's domain hostage. Annual domain cost is separate from my pricing (usually €10–15/year for .com)."
**A (SR)**: "[SR] Mogu da vas provedem kroz kupovinu domena ili to uradim za vas. Domen je uvek vaš. Godišnja cena domena je odvojena od mojih cena (obično €10–15/god za .com)."

#### Q9: Can I get an invoice?
**A (EN)**: "Yes, absolutely. I provide a proper invoice for every project. Everything clean and official."
**A (SR)**: "[SR] Da, apsolutno. Obezbeđujem uredan račun za svaki projekat. Sve čisto i zvanično."

#### Q10: How do we start working together?
**A (EN)**: "Simple. Message me on WhatsApp or fill out the contact form. Tell me roughly what you need. I'll get back to you within 24 hours with questions or a quote. No commitment until we both agree on the scope and price in writing."
**A (SR)**: "[SR] Jednostavno. Pišite mi na WhatsApp ili popunite kontakt formu. Kažite mi okvirno šta vam treba. Javim se u roku od 24 sata. Nema obaveze dok se oboje ne složimo o obimu i ceni u pisanom obliku."
